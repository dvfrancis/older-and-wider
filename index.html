Come in, sit down, and relax ...

Older & Wider is a cheeky peek into life's second act, served up with a cuppa and a hearty laugh by the hosts, and good friends, Jenny Eclair and Judith Holder. Their silver threads of wisdom weave through golden threads of laughter, extending a warm invite to a cosy chinwag.

Together, they share the delightful oddities and endearing eccentricities that clutter our lives as they navigate the rich tapestry of mature adulthood. It is also a celebration, where every wrinkle tells a story, every giggle is shared, and friends understand that life, like a fine wine, only gets better with age.

A new episode of the podcast is released every Friday.

Older & Wider was nominated for the British Podcast Awards - Listeners' Choice 2022.

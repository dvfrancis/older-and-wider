Relax and have fun with Older & Wider

Older & Wider is a cheeky peek into life's second act, served up with a cuppa and a hearty laugh by hosts, and good friends, Jenny Eclair and Judith Holder. Together, they share the delightful oddities and endearing eccentricities that clutter their lives as they navigate the rich tapestry of mature adulthood. It is also a celebration, where every wrinkle tells a story, every laugh is shared, and friends understand that life, like a fine wine, only gets better with age.

A new episode of the podcast is released every Friday.

Older & Wider was nominated for the British Podcast Awards - Listeners' Choice 2022.

What is Older & Wider?

Older & Wider is a weekly podcast where award-winning comedian, Jenny Eclair, and producer\writer, Judith Holder have a natter while putting the world to rights.

Originally aimed at the older woman, its fanbase has since expanded (can you see what we did there?) to include men and women, both young and "older".

From its launch in 2018, the podcast has logged over 250 episodes, and had over 5 and a half million downloads.

A recent run of live shows sold out within 48 hours and were so popular that additional dates had to be added to the tour (matinees only though because we all want to be home at a sensible time).

Who is Judith Holder?

Before Judith started presenting the Older & Wider podcast with Jenny she had a highly successful career as broadcaster, producer and writer for ITV and the BBC.

She has worked with many stars, including Dame Edna Everage, Clive James, and Joanna Lumley, and created “Grumpy Old Women”, which spawned a stage show that was seen around the world.

She has been BAFTA-nominated (twice), is a best-selling author, and originated the idea for this podcast.

Who is Jenny Eclair?

Jenny's talents encompass comedy, writing, and acting, and she was the first woman to win the Edinburgh Festival Perrier Award.

Her candid humour and caring nature have resonated with audiences around the world, with television appearances on shows such as “The Bill”, “Room 101”, and “Loose Women”.

More recently she was a popular contestant on shows including “I'm a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!” and “Taskmaster”.

While helping to create the highly successful show “Grumpy Old Women”, she became friends with television producer and writer, Judith Holder, with whom she now hosts the Older & Wider podcast.
